About Me

>Welcome to my portfolio! I am a skilled Full-Stack web developer with a strong passion for coding and developing highly functional web applications and websites. Hailing from Sitamarhi, Bihar, India, I hold a Diploma in Mechanical Engineering from IIMT Greater-Noida. My experience in Full-Stack web development from Masai has equipped me with the necessary skills to create both the front-end and back-end of web applications. I have a keen eye for detail and a strong focus on user experience, which enables me to create visually appealing and highly functional web applications that are both user-friendly and efficient. As a Full-Stack web developer, I am highly proficient in Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB stack, and have experience in building Full-Stack clones of existing web applications. I am highly motivated and enjoy taking on new challenges and solving complex problems, which has allowed me to create highly scalable and responsive web applications that are tailored to meet the specific needs of my clients. In addition to my technical expertise, I possess excellent communication and collaboration skills, which allow me to work effectively with clients and team members. I am committed to delivering high-quality work within the specified timelines and always strive to exceed client expectations. If you're looking for a talented and motivated Full-Stack web developer to take on your next web development project, please do not hesitate to get in touch. I would be more than happy to discuss your project requirements and how I can help you achieve your web development goals.
